# ZENDS Communications — Company Knowledge Base

## Company Overview

ZENDS Communications is a next-generation global telecom and digital services provider delivering mobile connectivity, broadband, enterprise networking, cloud infrastructure, IoT, cybersecurity, and digital platforms. ZENDS serves both individual consumers and enterprise customers across multiple regions, enabling personal communication, business operations, cloud computing, and smart-city services. ZENDS operates across consumer telecom, enterprise telecom, cloud infrastructure, smart city and IoT, and digital media and collaboration. ZENDS follows a service-oriented architecture in which every product is supported by digital platforms, billing engines, customer portals, and API-based provisioning systems.

## Company Policies

### Billing Policy
Monthly billing in advance. Enterprise customers receive consolidated invoices. Late payment after 7 days may suspend services.

### Refund Policy
Full refund within 7 days if usage is less than 10%. Cloud services are non-refundable after activation.

### Contract Policy
Individual users have no long-term lock-in. Enterprise customers have a minimum 12-month contract.

### SLA Policy
Individual users have 98.5% uptime, business users 99.5% uptime, and enterprise users 99.9% uptime.

### Data Privacy Policy
GDPR compliant, ISO 27001 certified, and encrypted data at rest and in transit.

### Fair Usage Policy
Unlimited plans are capped at 1TB per month.

### Support Tiers
Standard, Priority, and Enterprise Dedicated Support.

### Discount Policy
Bulk enterprise users can receive up to 30% discount. Annual payment discount is 15%.
